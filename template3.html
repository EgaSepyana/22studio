About InkThread

We're passionate about creating high-quality custom apparel that helps individuals and businesses express their unique identity.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, InkThread started as a small garage operation with a single screen printing press. Today, we've grown into a full-service custom apparel provider with state-of-the-art equipment and a team of passionate designers and printers.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Every shirt we print undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our high standards.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of printing techniques to bring your vision to life on fabric.

Screen Printing

Traditional screen printing for vibrant, long-lasting designs. Perfect for bulk orders with simple to complex designs.

  • Best for orders of 25+
  • Vibrant colors
  • Durable prints

DTG Printing

Direct-to-garment printing for photorealistic designs. Ideal for small orders or designs with many colors.

  • Great for 1-24 pieces
  • No color limitations
  • Soft hand feel

Embroidery

Premium embroidery services for a professional, upscale look. Perfect for logos and text on polos and hats.

  • Professional appearance
  • Long-lasting
  • Great for workwear

Custom Design

Our talented designers will work with you to create the perfect artwork for your custom apparel.

  • Professional designers
  • Unlimited revisions
  • Fast turnaround

Sublimation

Full-color, all-over printing for performance wear and light-colored garments.

  • Vibrant all-over prints
  • No cracking or peeling
  • Great for sports teams

Private Label

Create your own clothing line with custom labels, tags, and packaging.

  • Custom branding
  • White label service
  • Bulk discounts

How to Get Started

Our simple 5-step process makes getting custom t-shirts easy and stress-free.

1

Consultation

Contact us to discuss your project. We'll ask about quantity, design, colors, fabric preferences, and timeline.

2

Design

Submit your artwork or work with our designers. We'll provide mockups and recommendations for the best printing method.

3

Approval

Review and approve the final design mockup. We'll provide a detailed quote and production timeline.

4

Production

We print your shirts with care and precision. Quality checks are performed at every stage.

5

Delivery

Your custom shirts are packaged with care and shipped to your doorstep or ready for pickup.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's your minimum order quantity?

We can print as few as 1 shirt with our DTG technology. For screen printing, the minimum is typically 25 pieces.

How long does production take?

Standard turnaround is 7-10 business days after approval. Rush services are available for an additional fee.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! Our design team can create custom artwork for your shirts starting at $50 per design.

What file formats do you accept for artwork?

We prefer vector files (AI, EPS, PDF) but can also work with high-resolution PNG, JPG, or PSD files.
