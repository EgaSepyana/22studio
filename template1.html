About PrintWave

Founded in 2010, PrintWave has grown from a small garage operation to one of the most respected screen printing businesses in the region. Our team of skilled artisans combines traditional techniques with modern technology to deliver exceptional results.

We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, commitment to quality, and dedication to customer satisfaction. Whether you need 10 shirts or 10,000, we treat every order with the same level of care and professionalism.

Our Process

Simple steps from concept to finished product

Consultation

We start by understanding your needs, budget, and timeline. Our experts will guide you through material choices, design options, and printing techniques.

1
2

Design & Artwork

Our design team works with you to create or refine your artwork. We ensure it's print-ready and optimized for the best results on your chosen materials.

Screen Preparation

Each color in your design gets its own screen. We coat, expose, and wash screens with precision to capture every detail of your artwork.

3
4

Printing

Our skilled printers apply each color layer by layer with perfect registration. We monitor quality throughout the entire print run.

Curing & Finishing

Prints are heat-cured for durability, then inspected, folded, and packaged. We conduct final quality checks before delivery.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our screen printing services

Our minimum order quantity is 24 pieces for most apparel items. For promotional products and specialty items, minimums may vary. We can accommodate smaller quantities for sample orders or test prints.

Standard turnaround time is 7-10 business days from approval of artwork. Rush services are available for an additional fee, with options for 3-day or 5-day turnaround. Complex designs or large orders may require more time.

Absolutely! Our in-house design team can create custom artwork based on your ideas, refine existing designs, or provide templates to get you started. Design services start at $75/hour with flat-rate packages available for common projects.

We prefer vector files (AI, EPS, PDF) with all text converted to outlines. We also accept high-resolution (300dpi) raster files (PSD, TIFF, PNG). For best results, artwork should be in CMYK color mode and include all fonts used.

Yes! We offer water-based inks (which are softer and more eco-friendly than plastisol), organic cotton garments, and recycled materials. Our facility follows sustainable practices including waste reduction and responsible chemical disposal.
